The problem is a translation gap. Clinicians speak naturally, but E-H-Rs need structured data with I-C-D-10 codes and R-X-Norm identifiers. Manual data entry is slow, error-prone, and burns out physicians.

Let me show you the working system. I'll start the server with Docker Compose. The server is now running.

Here's a cardiology consultation. A 48 year old male with chest pain, on aspirin. The physician orders a stress test and starts statin therapy.

I'll send this to the extraction A-P-I. In under three seconds, Med-Gemma extracts the complete clinical picture.
